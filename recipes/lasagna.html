Mom and Dad's Lasagna

Description

A deliciously cheesy dish for the family. Easily serves four.

Ingredients:

  • 9 Lasagna noodles
  • 1lb ground beef
  • At least 2 cups shredded mozzerella, colby, parmesan, whatever shredded cheese you like
  • 1 jar prego meat sauce
  • 1 regular sized tub of cottage cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit for an hour and a half
  2. Ground beef
  3. Soften noodles
  4. Drain water used to soften noodles
  5. Mix meat and sauce in pan, heat to boiling
  6. Add cottage cheese to mix
  7. Build in layers; lay 3 noodles down side by side in casserole dish, followed by a layer of meat sauce, 1/3 cup of cheese mix, leaving most of the cheese for the top at the end.
  8. Repeat step 7 two more times
  9. Pull out dish with 15 minutes left and add more cheese mix to the top because it tends to dry out
  10. Enjoy
